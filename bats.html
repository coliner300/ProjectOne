All About Bats

Aluminum Bats

In baseball there are 2 types of bats, one is aluminum which we use from when we are little until the end of college. These bats make the ball go farther than any other type of bat and a high end aluminum bat goes for $500.

Wood Bats

The second type of bat is wood. You only use this type of bat when you get to MLB. These are harder to hit with than an aluminum bat, and hitting with these show the true skill a player has. A good wood bat can cost anywhere from $120 to $300.

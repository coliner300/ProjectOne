All About Gloves


A glove is made of leather. The type of leather changes based on location. A glove is the most important part of baseball and cannot be played without it.

These are the glove brands

  • Wilson
  • Easton
  • Louisville Slugger
  • Mizuno
  • Nokona
  • Rawlings
  • Spalding
  • Akadema
